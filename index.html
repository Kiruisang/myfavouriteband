Band

Big Time Rush is a boy band that was created in 2009 and it inluded the four young men.They grew to fame almost immediately and started giving already existent boy bands a run for their money. The whole team just had the chemistry of a lively lovely band not forgetting their producer Stephen Glickman or as widely known Gustavo.And by his side, the ever present Kelly who was always full of life and joy and Arthur who was the company executive. This was baasically the whole team

why BTR ?

I was introduced to the band as a television series as they apparently managed to run both careers concurrently. They made me smile, laugh and want to live like them, I enjoyed the show and let their music take me to places since I felt like I understood them and why they were singing what they were singing.
