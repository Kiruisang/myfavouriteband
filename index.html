About

Maroon 5 is a very famous american band known mainly for their pop sensations.The band is one of the oldest standing bands as it was formed in 1994 while they were till in high school and were originally called Kara's Flowers. The group re-emerged in 2001 as Maroon 5 after they had stopped releasing music to focus on their studies.They went on to win a multiple grammies to date with worldwide recognizable songs. The band sings songs that are so relatable to me and they are such a simple group who have had the heart to stick together through it all. They are also fun.
